Loot 'Em

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add an unknown number of points to your score.

To win, match your score to the random number; if your score goes above the given number, you lose the loot. All of your hardwon crystals are gone. The chill in the cavern nips at your soul as you watch the jewels' glinting shimmer fade into the depths below. A draft blows; it's best to move on. There's no more for you here.
